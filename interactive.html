Water is essential for all forms of life, covering about 71% of the Earth's surface and making up a significant portion of our bodies. It exists in three main states: liquid, solid (ice), and gas (vapor), each playing a crucial role in Earth's climate and weather systems. Without water, ecosystems would collapse, and human survival would be at risk.
